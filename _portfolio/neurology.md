As **Project Lead** starting in October 2025, I am leading an ambitious medical AI project at Kumoh National Institute of Technology.

Our goal is to accurately predict neurological prognoses for patients suffering from post-cardiac arrest brain injuries. This automated model analyzes clinical and imaging data to assist medical professionals in making faster, more context-aware decisions.

**Technologies**: Medical Imaging Processing, Deep Learning, Transformers, AI in Healthcare.
