As a **Project Lead**, I spearheaded the research and integration of a precision medical platform utilizing radiogenomics data.

This project focuses on **Radiogenomics-Driven Hierarchical Multimodal Stacking** for the non-invasive triage of high-risk PKD1-truncating genotypes in Autosomal Dominant Polycystic Kidney Disease (ADPKD). 

Our work successfully combined patient profile data, radiological imaging, and genomic profiles to provide precise diagnostics.

**Technologies**: Deep Learning, PyTorch, Multi-modal architectures, SimpleITK, NiBabel
**Achievements**: 
* KCI Paper Publication & Patent (Sep 2025)
* MICCAI submission (Under Review, Feb 2026)
