As **Project Lead** (Sep 2024 - Dec 2024) at Kumoh National Institute of Technology, I managed the development of a real-time deep learning-based environmental monitoring model.

The primary objective was to ensure ultra-precise stability in manufacturing environments, targeting fluctuations within **±0.1℃** for temperature and **±1%RH** for humidity. 

**Technologies**: Python, Deep Learning, MLOps, Time-series analysis.
