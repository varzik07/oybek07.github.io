Hello! I am **Oybek Valiyev**, an AI Engineer and Researcher with a deep passion for **Medical Imaging, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision**.

Currently based in South Korea, I work as a Researcher at the **Kumoh National Institute of Technology**, where I lead AI-related projects focusing on healthcare applications. My research aims to bridge the gap between advanced artificial intelligence and practical medical diagnostics, specifically through the development of precision medicine platforms and patient prognosis prediction models.

### Focus Areas
* **Medical Imaging & Radiomics**: Developing multimodal deep learning models for disease classification (like ADPKD) and triage using genomic and radiological data.
* **Computer Vision**: Leveraging CNNs, Vision Transformers, and Generative AI (GANs, Diffusion Models) for complex image analysis.
* **Large Language Models & MLOps**: Integrating RAG pipelines and modern tooling (Docker, Gradio, FastAPI) to build end-to-end intelligent systems.

I hold an M.S. in Software Engineering from Kumoh National Institute of Technology and a B.S. in Economics from Chonnam National University. Prior to my current research role, I gained extensive experience as a Project Manager leading software developments and as a Tech Specialized Translator/Interpreter for Computer Science coursework.
